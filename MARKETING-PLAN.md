# Cascade EV Marketing Plan

## Best starting focus
Lead with rentals first, especially the 2020 Model X Long Range Plus and the 2026 AWD Model Y. Use Turo for transaction trust and this site for brand, search traffic, and local authority.

## Best starting locations
1. **Portland** — biggest search volume, airport traffic, leisure and business travelers.
2. **Hood River / Columbia Gorge** — strong fit for scenic drives, weddings, adventure stays, and premium weekend travel.
3. **Mount Hood corridor** — ideal for destination rentals, lodging partnerships, photo shoots, ski season, and luxury-stay add-ons.

## Positioning
- Not a generic rental fleet
- Not a generic used car lot
- Premium Oregon EV experience with Tesla expertise

## Best acquisition channels
### 1. Turo listing optimization
- Strong lead image
- Clear pickup and delivery language
- Reassuring first-time EV copy
- Emphasize local trip use cases: Mount Hood, Gorge, Portland weekends, weddings, business travel

### 2. Local SEO
Target pages and terms:
- Tesla rental Portland
- Tesla rental Hood River
- Mount Hood Tesla rental
- Model X rental Oregon
- Model Y rental Oregon
- EV rental Columbia Gorge

### 3. Partnership marketing
Best partnerships:
- Boutique hotels and lodges near Mount Hood
- Wedding venues and planners in Hood River and the Gorge
- Vacation rentals and property managers
- Photographers and elopement vendors
- Local business travel hosts and executive assistants

### 4. Content strategy
Best early content:
- Best Tesla rental for Mount Hood trips
- Model X vs Model Y rental in Oregon
- What it is like to drive a Tesla in the Columbia Gorge
- First-time Tesla renter guide for Portland visitors

### 5. Review flywheel
- Use early Turo reviews as trust content on the site
- Add guest quotes to the home and rental pages
- Convert positive guest feedback into FAQ and service copy

## Expansion sequence
1. Rentals
2. Repeat rentals and reviews
3. Add waitlist for future sales
4. Add owner help and onboarding service
5. Expand into curated used EV sales
